Welcome to Tokyo

About Tokyo

Tokyo is the capital of Japan and one of the most vibrant and futuristic cities in the world. It combines traditional culture with cutting-edge technology and pop culture.

Top Attractions

Tokyo Tower

A symbol of Tokyo offering panoramic views of the city skyline.

Shibuya Crossing

Experience the world’s busiest pedestrian crossing at the heart of Tokyo’s youth culture.

Senso-ji Temple

Tokyo’s oldest temple, located in the historic district of Asakusa.

Famous Food

Sushi

Fresh and expertly prepared sushi from Tsukiji Market.

Ramen

Rich and savory noodle soup with many regional varieties.

Takoyaki

Delicious octopus-filled balls, a must-try Tokyo street food.
